Human Rights.

Everyone has the right to be treated equally and not to be discriminated against in employment and the provision of services and facilities to which members of the public have access. Whether you have been treated unfairly because of your race, color, sexual orientation or other characteristics, passed over for a job or promotion, we will assist you in your human rights claim. Pamela Large Moran has over ten years experience acting in the role of a Mediator for Human Rights Commissions in Canada.
